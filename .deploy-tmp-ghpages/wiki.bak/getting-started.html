Getting Started

Your first steps on the ZION TestNet.

What is ZION?

ZION is the first consciousness-based blockchain. It combines conventional cryptography with a humanitarian mission and a novel mining model that multiplies rewards based on spiritual development levels.

Consciousness Mining

Miners can receive multipliers (1x–15x) according to 9 levels from Physical to ON_THE_STAR. While the mechanism is in active research on the testnet, the intent is to encourage ethical, community-oriented contribution.

First Steps

  1. Download TestNet Client
    Get the latest binaries from Releases.
  2. Run a Node
    ./zion-node --testnet --rpc-port 8545
  3. Set Up a Wallet
    Create/backup your keys; then connect your miner or interact via RPC. See Docs → Setup.
