Consciousness Levels

Nine levels of spiritual development and their mining multipliers.

The Nine Levels

  1. Level 1 — Physical: Focus on material and bodily needs. Multiplier: 1×
  2. Level 2 — Emotional: Awareness of feelings and interpersonal connections. Multiplier: 2×
  3. Level 3 — Intellectual: Rational thinking, analysis, and knowledge acquisition. Multiplier: 3×
  4. Level 4 — Intuitive: Deeper insights beyond logic; inner knowing. Multiplier: 4×
  5. Level 5 — Mental: Mastery of thought patterns and mental discipline. Multiplier: 5×
  6. Level 6 — Spiritual: Connection to higher consciousness and universal principles. Multiplier: 7×
  7. Level 7 — Cosmic: Unity with cosmic energies and collective awareness. Multiplier: 9×
  8. Level 8 — Galactic: Expanded consciousness across galactic dimensions. Multiplier: 12×
  9. Level 9 — On The Star: Highest achievable state, stellar consciousness. Multiplier: 15×

Multipliers

Each consciousness level grants a mining reward multiplier. Higher levels incentivize personal growth and ethical community contribution. The mechanism is under active research on the testnet.

Validation (Future)

Detailed definitions and validation criteria for consciousness levels will be refined during testnet. The goal is to ethically motivate community participation without creating unfair advantages.

Consciousness mining is an experimental feature. Feedback and community input are essential for its development.
