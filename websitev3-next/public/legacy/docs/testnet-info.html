Network Specifications

  • Network: ZION TestNet 2.8.5
  • Block time: 150 seconds (~2.5 minutes)
  • Block reward: ~5,479.45 ZION (subject to distribution rules)
  • Total supply: 144,000,000,000 ZION (long-term issuance ~50 years)
